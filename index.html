Experience Productivity

Keep projects on track and meet shared goals.

Save important information for youself, tag critical information for your team, and start new discussions to keep projects on track and tackle shared goals.

Send secure messages to other collaborators.

Collaborate professionally with internal team members and external partners. Send group messages with the ability to react, respond, edit, and delete.

Share files with confidence.

Select who has access to which files and set permission levels on a person-by-person basis.

Keep personal and organizational data secure.

Oversee how you want to handle data at the end of a discussion. Delete data from alll servers or lock and store data long-term. You decide how to best keep infomration safe.

Feature Privacy

Work seamlessly
with external partners.

Monoly helps you communicate professionally and share files securely with internalteammates and external partners. Send secure messages and protected files to people and organizations you trust.

Know where your data lives.

Whether working internally or externally, Monoly helps you control who has access to what data and choose where data gets stored. Save and exchange data from your organization’s servers, free from 3rd party surveillance.

Harness the power of
Monoly's enterprise blockchain.

Monoly’s proprietary enterprise blockchain infrastructure goes far beyond yesterday’s blockchain. Monoly connects and verifies organizations’ servers without ever collecting data. Use Monoly as your link to the future of secure data exchange.
